About Me

Hi, my name is James Waite and I am a recent graduate from the Cleveland Institute of Art, where I earned a Bachelors Degree of Fine Art from the Drawing Department as well as an emphasis in Visual Culture, Art, and History.

My artistic practice has lead me to seek avenues of participation and to explore digital culture. I believe that becoming a web developer will give me the skills to succeed in the industry as well as to further my artistic projects.
